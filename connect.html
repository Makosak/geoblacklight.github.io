Connect with the GeoBlacklight Community

GeoBlacklight is more than just software. It's a community. There are lots of different ways to connect and get involved

  • Join the Google Group

    Join the GeoBlacklight Google Group to follow general project discussions and feature announcements.

  • Chat on Slack

    Join the GeoBlacklight Slack channel to chat with others in the community and to work through technical questions. Sign up here.

  • Monthly Community Meeting

    The GeoBlacklight Community Meeting takes place on the second Wednesday of every month. Everyone is welcome to join!

  • Share Metadata

    GeoBlacklight gets even better when organizations share metadata. Start contributing your metadata to OpenGeoMetadata or use it to populate your own GeoBlacklight installation.

  • Discuss on GitHub

    Do you have an bug you want to report? Is documentation missing or unclear? Would you like to suggest a new feature? Anyone can create an issue on GeoBlacklight's GitHub issue tracker.
